Molloy College

This fall I just started at Molloy College. I absolutely love it here. I'm on the Women's Rugby team and I see my teammates and my friends all the time on campus. It's a huge adjustent from high school but I definitely like college a lot better than I liked high school. When I was looking at schools last years I went to a few local schools but Molloy stood out to me. There is a huge sense of community here. I also liked the small class sizes and the location. There were a lot of factrs that made me realize Molloy was the right choice for me.
